Rules & Guidelines

These simple guidelines help us prepare fresh food on time and serve everyone smoothly.

  • Advance Booking: Meals must be booked at least one day in advance.
  • Delivery Window: Meals are delivered during fixed lunch hours on working days.
  • Meal Pass Validity: Meal passes are valid for 30 days from the date of purchase.
  • Skipped Meals: If a meal is not booked for a day, it will be considered skipped and cannot be carried forward.
  • Personal Use: Meal passes and subscriptions are meant for individual use and are non-transferable.
  • No Refunds: Once purchased, meals, passes, and subscriptions are non-refundable.
  • Menu Changes: Menu items may vary occasionally to maintain freshness and quality.
  • Service Area: Delivery is available only in selected office locations.

We keep these guidelines simple so you can enjoy your lunch without confusion. If you ever have questions, feel free to message us.

Meal passes allow you to buy multiple meals in advance at a lower price. You can use them flexibly within the validity period by booking one day in advance.
